# What are Up/Down Options?

Up/Down options are a type of derivative contract that turns every trade into a simple 'Up' or 'Down' question â€“ 
you decide whether a market is likely to be above a certain price, at a certain time. 
If you think it will be, you open a 'Up' position. If you think it wonâ€™t be, you open a 'Down' position.

:::info
 For instance, a trade could be created on the following prediction: 
 
 ETH will be above (Up) 1.8k USD 15 mins later

When the option expires (i.e. 15 mins later):
- if the outcome of the above prediction is true, then the trader makes a profit. 
- If the outcome of the above statement is false, then the trader takes a loss.
:::

## Why Trade Up/Down Options?
Up/Down options offer a fantastic way of trading price action, 
hedging high leveraged positions and balancing your exposure to specific assets. 
It is a preferred insttruments for traders looking to avoid the complexities and 
heightened risks associated with perpetual contracts, such as funding rates, potential for liquidation, and uncapped risk.
